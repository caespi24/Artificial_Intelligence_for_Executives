Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming various industries, enabling them to innovate and restructure. Given AI's profound impact on our personal and professional lives, it's crucial for executives and decision-makers to stay informed about the latest developments in AI. The seminar discussed will offer practical insights applicable across numerous industries, aiding organizations to remain competitive by reskilling and upskilling their staff. It will guide attendees on constructing AI using appropriate hardware, software, and algorithms, and showcase pre-built applications and software development kits (SDKs) that save time and effort. This event is particularly valuable as it makes AI more accessible to those with limited programming skills, thus lowering the entry barriers to implementing AI in various projects and enterprises.

  • Who Should Attend / Participate

  • Executives and Managers

    who want to respond to new opportunities in AI, entrepreneurs, start-up owners of AI businesses.

  • Decision-Makers

    who recognize the importance of AI and responsible for shaping the organization's digital transformation strategy.

  • IT professionals

    who are interested harnessing the power of AI in their field.

  • Tech Enthusiasts

    who are interested and recognize that AI will play a big role in their life.
