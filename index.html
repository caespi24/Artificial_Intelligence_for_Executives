    Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming various industries, enabling them to innovate and restructure. Given AI's profound impact on our personal and professional lives, it's crucial for executives and decision-makers to stay informed about the latest developments in AI. The seminar will offer practical insights applicable across numerous industries, aiding organizations to remain competitive by reskilling and upskilling their staff. It will guide attendees on constructing AI using appropriate hardware, software, and algorithms, and showcase pre-built applications and software development kits (SDKs) that save time and effort. This event is particularly valuable as it makes AI more accessible to those with limited programming skills, thus lowering the entry barriers to implementing AI in various projects and enterprises.
